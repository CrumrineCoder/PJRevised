Cookies made with love by a mother and daughter locally in New York City

Hi, I'm Penny Jane May!

Me and mom are always ready to make cookies to spread love to classmates or strangers. We bake them ourselves but I like the decorating part better. Every holiday we make a fresh batch or whenever one of my or mom's friends have an event. Santa loved my reindeer cookies so maybe you'll like them too! I laugh everytime I get to hand out the cookies and see frowns turn upside down.
